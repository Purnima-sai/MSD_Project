Spaced Repetition System

Our algorithm uses the forgetting curve to determine the perfect time to review each flashcard, ensuring you spend time only on what you're about to forget.

  • Based on proven cognitive science research
  • Adjusts to your individual forgetting patterns
  • Optimizes study time by focusing on weak areas
  • Can improve retention by up to 400%

Active Recall Practice

Retrieving information from memory strengthens neural connections far more than passive review, making it one of the most effective study techniques.

  • Forces your brain to reconstruct knowledge
  • Identifies gaps in your understanding
  • Builds stronger, longer-lasting memories
  • More effective than re-reading or highlighting
