Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlashBoost

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and for institutional plans, we also accept bank transfers and purchase orders.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period, and you'll retain access to all Pro features until then.

Is there a student discount available?

Yes! Students can get 50% off the Pro plan with a valid .edu email address or student ID verification. Contact our support team for more information.

How does the free trial work?

Our free trial gives you full access to all Pro features for 14 days. No credit card is required to start the trial. At the end of the trial period, you can choose to upgrade or continue with the free plan.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the change will take effect at your next billing date.
